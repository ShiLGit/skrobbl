skrobbl is a word-guessing web game inspired by skribbl.io

Win condition: have the most points at the end of the game. The way you earn points depends on your role for your current round:

Typer (role): one per round. Choose one of three words for other players to guess. The game ends when every player has had a turn as the typer. You will receive points every time their word is guessed correctly.

Guesser (role): guess the word the typer has chosen. The earlier you make a correct guess, the higher your score.

There are two types of hints that typers can give to guessers to help them:

Picture hints: an array of twelve buttons will be populated with random words at the beginning of the round. The typer will pick any word-buttons that are relevant to the secret word as a hint to the guessers.

Word hints: the typer may type sentence hints for guessers to see. However, these hints are run through a language-translation service (?) multiple times to muddle their meaning.
